Capabilities

Built for Clinical Settings

Designed to integrate into real-world radiology and emergency workflows.

🌐
Universal Device Support
Works on any device with a browser — Windows, macOS, iOS, Android (including Xiaomi, Huawei, Samsung). No app installation required.
📷
Camera Integration
On mobile devices, directly capture X-ray images from the device camera or PACS viewer screenshots for instant analysis.
🔁
Test-Time Augmentation
5× TTA including flips, crops, and rotations reduces prediction variance and improves overall accuracy compared to single-pass inference.
⚖️
Balanced Class Training
WeightedRandomSampler and Label Smoothing (0.1) address class imbalance, ensuring reliable detection across both rare and common fracture types.
📊
Probability Distribution
Full probability output for all 10 classes. Visualize differential confidence, not just the top prediction, for more nuanced clinical interpretation.
📋
Downloadable Report
Generate a structured clinical report with prediction details, confidence scores, and timestamp — ready to attach to patient records.
Clinical Workflow

How to Use

A simple 4-step process designed to complement existing radiology workflows without disruption.

1
Acquire radiograph
Obtain a standard AP or lateral X-ray of the area of interest. JPEG export from PACS or a digital photo of the film both work. Resolution above 224×224px recommended.
2
Upload to FractureAI
Drag-and-drop the image, or tap "Upload" on mobile to open the file picker or camera. The system accepts all major image formats.
3
Review AI analysis
Within seconds, the ensemble model produces a primary classification with confidence percentage and full probability distribution across all 10 fracture types.
4
Confirm with specialist
Use the AI output as decision support alongside clinical history and physical findings. Final diagnosis remains the responsibility of a qualified clinician.
